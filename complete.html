Intro About Me

From a young age, I had an interest in all aspects of design. In fact, for most of my childhood I wanted to be an architect as well as an interior designer. Then, at the age of 15, I discovered the world of web design & development. Everything changed.

Now, almost 8 years later, I work full time for the awesome team at Google. They kindly took me under their wing just over a year ago and I plan to stay there for the foreseeable future.
